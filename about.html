Origin Story

Our lovely restaurant was first opened in San Francisco over 50 years ago. Since then we have expanded to several locations in the United States. Our restaurants feature homestyle recipes that have been passed down for generations. Be sure to bring your family and friends as this restaurant will be a truly unforgettable experience.
